# Titan Fitness — Update (March 27, 2026)

## Program Mode

- **Weekly workout programs**: Switch between "Daily" (single AI-generated workout) and "Program" (full 7-day plan) in your profile settings. Programs include a mix of workout styles with built-in rest days for balanced training.
- **Program detail view**: Browse your entire week at a glance with expandable day cards showing every exercise, set, and rep target.
- **Edit exercises within a program**: Tap any exercise in a program day to swap, replace, or modify it using AI-powered quick actions — no need to regenerate the whole program.
- **Configurable active days**: Choose how many active workout days (3–6) your program should include. Great if weekends are off-limits or you prefer more rest days.

## Workout Preferences

- **Average workout duration**: Set your preferred workout length in minutes. When configured, AI-generated workouts will be designed to fit within your target time.
- **Count-in timer**: Optional 3, 5, or 7-second countdown before timed exercises begin, with audio beeps on each count. Skippable at any time — just like the rest timer.
- **Grouped settings**: Rest timer sound, workout mode, duration, and count-in are now organized together under Workout Preferences in your profile.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where the workout completion screen wouldn't appear after finishing a workout.
- Fixed iOS PWA safe area handling on the program detail header.
- Improved AI workout generation reliability.
